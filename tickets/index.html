Frequently Asked Questions

What are "Workshops" and what does it mean to "Never End"?

This is a con focused on the most interactive and skill-building parts - practical training. Your ticket provides full access to a wealth of courses we call “workshops” that will cover all domains for this conference. They will all be practical and for all learner levels.

In the coming months we will reveal the selected finalists for conference workshop content!

We will also choose some workshops to have a live presentation as part of the training so you can see the workshop creator live in action and deliver a portion of it during the conference days.

This will feel like multiple courses in one. Your tier of access provides how long you can access the conference workshop content.

Conference workshop content will be from the following domains:

  • DFIR
  • Detection Engineering
  • Reverse Engineering
  • Threat Hunting
  • Malware Analysis
  • Cyber Threat Intelligence
  • SecOps
  • Tactical GRC
How do I access the conference

The conference will consist of hosted VMs and lab guides. The conference will be live-streamed on Twitch, Youtube, and LinkedIn for you to follow along and engage.

Additionally, you can get help, support, and interact with other attendees in the ContinuumCon Discord server.

Do I need any special software or VMs to join?

All you need is browser access and a stable internet connection as everything is hosted for you. Optionally, you can access your VMs via a VPN.
