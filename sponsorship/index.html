Frequently Asked Questions

How do I become a sponsor?

Click the “Contact Us” button on this page and we’ll get back to you with sponsorship options and next steps.

Can we customize a sponsorship package?

Yes — we welcome custom packages. Tell us your objectives and we’ll propose options tailored to your audience and budget.

Are there speaking opportunities for sponsors?

Yes, in the Champion package, with the following caveats and consideration:

This conference is designed to be vendor neutral as possible. With that said, a proposed presentation can touch on tradecraft that correlates to a market solution so as long as the use, access, and direct demo of the solution is not the focus of the presentation.

You may propose a challenge and show others how to work the problem in a hands-on workshop and may reference the tool/solution as something that could help resolve this at scale, but the lab and presentation will not focus on that solution as a core point of discussion.

What is the On-Demand Presentation and Workshop?

Sposors at this package level will have a dedicated lab and corresponding on-demand video in the conference for attendees to engage with and follow olong at their own pace.

What platforms host the stream?

We stream live on YouTube, Twitch, LinkedIn, X and On-Demand content is hosted on YouTube and within the conference
